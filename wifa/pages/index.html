Priority Areas

Women can't rely on male-run technology. Our work is focused on
building a resilient internet infastructure for the Women's Movement.

Social Media Censorship

Twitter, Reddit, Youtube, and nearly every major social media site have been silencing women. We're fighting back.

Web Deplatforming

Internet giants like Google have used their power to shut down feminist spaces. We're creating resilient solutions.

Training and Education

Women and girls are lagging behind in internet literacy and programming. WIFA is committed to building up the next generation of female technologists.

Internet Security

Men pose a threat to women and girls online. All of our projects are held to the highest security standards to protect women.

Volunteer Organizing

We help our partner organizations get access to female technologists who want to help the movement.

Material Support

We create jobs for female technologists ourside of the patriachal tech ecosystem, so women can forge their own future.

About Us

Women's Internet Freedom Alliance (WIFA) was founded in 2021 by M. K. Fain. WIFA provides support and capacity building for technology projects that advance women's rights, especially in establishing resilience against male control of internet resources.

WIFA is fiscally sponsored by the Women's Liberation Front, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in the United States.
